Since there is room for love in that small workshop, where art began between earthen walls with a roof full of holes through which the sun's rays entered, hard hands, a firm back and great ingenuity were the participants in creating textiles. Craftsmanship is a legacy for our culture. On this website you can see the products, we have on stock and see the new updates. Creating this website, it's an easier way for my clients to see the products I have and know what we do as indigenous community. This website will cover mostly handmade clothing from my hometown, Otavalo. You can find here information about the materials used for the clothing and the process of the product. You can browser the products and see the different materials, colors, and patterns we have. Also, you can choose the elements and see the variety we have.
